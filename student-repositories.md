## 📁 Structure

Each link leads to a student repository containing:

- Lab 1 – MITRE ATT&CK Mapping  
- Lab 2 – Simple Anomaly Detection

Content varies by group depending on chosen datasets, threat report,  
pipeline configuration, and selected project topic.

---

## 📝 Notes

- Groups maintain their repositories independently.  
- Repositories are public to encourage peer learning.  
- Students are encouraged to explore work from other groups to compare  
  approaches, data sources, and implementation choices.
